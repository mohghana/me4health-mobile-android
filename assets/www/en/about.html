Health Training Schools in Ghana

ME4H (Mobile Education for Health) is part of a series of e-learning tools being used by USAIDís flagship Maternal and Child Survival Program (MCSP) to provide learners access to evidence based health resources, focused on the components around strengthening and building the capacity of pre-service institutions.
